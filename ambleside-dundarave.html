Ambleside and Dundarave are the heart of West Vancouver — walkable villages, a mix of 1940s character homes, mid-century houses and sleek new builds, many with tucked-away or laneway garages. That variety means everything from original wood doors to the latest glass-and-aluminum, and we service all of them.

What we see in these neighbourhoods

  • Older wood doors that swell and stick in the wet season
  • Tight, lane-access garages that need careful track work
  • Heritage and character homes wanting carriage-style replacements
  • Modern Marine Drive builds wanting full-view glass
  • Salt-air corrosion on cables and springs near the seawall

Close to the water = faster wear

A block from the Dundarave or Ambleside seawall, salt air corrodes springs and cables noticeably faster. We fit corrosion-resistant hardware and recommend an annual tune-up to catch rust before it strands you.
