New Garage Door Installation in West Vancouver

Your garage door is up to a third of your home's street view. We help you choose the right style, colour and insulation for the West Coast, then install it flawlessly.

From Dundarave character homes to glass-and-cedar new builds in the British Properties, the right garage door transforms a façade. We measure, supply and install doors that suit West Vancouver architecture and stand up to coastal weather — and we handle the old-door removal and disposal.

Door styles we install

  • Modern full-view — aluminum frame with frosted or clear glass; the signature West Coast contemporary look
  • Carriage house — warm wood-grain with decorative hardware; perfect for traditional and craftsman homes
  • Traditional raised-panel steel — timeless, durable and the best value
  • Contemporary flush — clean, minimal, no lines; pairs with concrete-and-cedar builds

Insulation matters on the North Shore

Many West Van garages sit under living space or double as gyms and studios. An insulated door (polyurethane core, higher R-value) keeps that space comfortable, cuts noise from rain and wind, and adds rigidity so the door lasts longer. We'll match the R-value to how you use the space.

How the process works

  • Free in-home measure & design consult
  • Clear, written quote with styles and colours
  • Order & schedule (most doors arrive in 2–4 weeks)
  • Professional one-day installation + opener setup
  • Walkthrough, balance test and warranty
